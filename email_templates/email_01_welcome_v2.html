Your Next Steps

1

Enter Your Baseline Data

Log into the MacroFit app and complete your baseline questionnaire. This is how we calculate your personal Macro Points budget. Don't skip this step — your plan can't be generated without it. Try to get this done a few days before your start date.
 
2

Read Your Emails

Over the next several days, you'll receive a series of emails walking you through exactly how MacroFit works. Read them — they'll make your first week so much easier.
 
3

Your Meal Plan Opens ~1 Week Before Your Start Date

You'll get a reminder when it's time to build your Week 1 meal plan, create your grocery list, and prep. Until then, get your baseline data entered and get familiar with the app.
