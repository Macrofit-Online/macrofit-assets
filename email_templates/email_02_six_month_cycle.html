MacroFit is designed around a 6-month cycle. Your Macro Points are adjusted weekly based on your progress and adherence, cycling your calories strategically over 24 weeks.

The reverse diet phase is critical to your long-term success. Your calories can't just go down endlessly — they must be brought back up so your metabolism stays healthy and you can diet back down again in the future.

Most MacroFitters don't even notice the reverse diet happening. They continue losing body fat for the majority of it. That's by design.
