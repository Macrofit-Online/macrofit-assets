The Weekend

Here's how to stay on track

This is typically where people get derailed. A little strategy goes a long way.

Pick your one off-plan meal and stick to it. Going out with friends? Birthday party? Family function? Great — that can be your "free" meal. Don't be afraid to pack an ice chest if you're traveling. A little preparation beats feeling like you have no options. And don't forget about your On The Go meals — they're made for exactly these situations.

If you find yourself somewhere you can't have your meal prep, it's not a free meal, and you have no OTG options — stick to these three rules:

1

Prioritize lean protein. Choose grilled chicken breast or filet whenever possible. Restaurant meats can add a surprising amount of fat, so leaner choices give you much more flexibility.

2

Keep carbs to about a palm-sized serving. Rice, potatoes, bread, pasta — use your hand as a guide. Your MacroFit meals often contain more carbs, but those meals have controlled fat. Restaurant meals usually don't. More hidden fat means you generally need less carbohydrate.

3

Fill up on free veggies. Just be mindful of how they're prepared. If they taste unbelievably delicious... they're probably swimming in butter or oil. 😅 Eat them — just don't rely on them being "free" unless they're plainly prepared.

The first couple of weekends are usually the hardest. But every time you make a choice that aligns with your goals, you're strengthening that discipline muscle. It gets easier.

I can honestly say I've never had someone tell me they regretted sticking to their plan after the weekend was over.
