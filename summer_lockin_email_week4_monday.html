The only comparison that matters.

Hey {{contact.first_name}},

Before we get into the challenge, I want to say something I think you need to hear right now.

Comparison is the thief of joy — and in a fitness journey, it's also the thief of progress.

It's easy at this point to look at someone else's results and wonder why yours don't look the same. To scroll the FB group and see someone's win and think "why isn't that happening for me?" To compare where you are right now to where someone else is after months or years of consistency.

Your journey is yours. Your starting point, your body, your history, your hormones, your stress levels, your sleep, your life — none of it is the same as anyone else in this challenge. Comparing your progress to someone else's is like comparing apples to something that isn't even a fruit.

The only comparison that matters is you on Day 1 versus you right now.

Week 4 Challenge

This week

2 cups of veggies every day.

✅

Do It

2 cups of veggies every day this week. Add them to what you're already eating — don't overthink it.

📲

Share It

Your non-scale victory. Not the number on the scale — everything else. Energy. Sleep. How your clothes fit. A decision you made automatically that used to take effort. A workout that felt easier than it did a few weeks ago. That's your real data. Share it in the FB group.

Why veggies this week? Volume and fiber. Two cups of vegetables adds volume to your meals without adding significant calories — which means you feel fuller, more satisfied, and naturally less likely to go looking for something else. It also supports digestion, energy, and blood sugar regulation. A small habit with a big impact on how you feel day to day.
